Pizza

Description

Classic recipe for a perfect 24 hour fermentation fluffy, crispy, amazing Pizza Napoletana

Use slap and fold technique and bake on a steel or a pizza oven for the best home made pizza of your life

You're welcome

Ingredients

  • Flour - 1 kg
  • Salt - 28 grams
  • Water - 700 grams
  • Yeast - 0,3 grams

Steps

  1. Mix yeast and salt into the water with your fingers until completely dissolved
  2. Pour all the flour into the water and mix with your hands until the mix is smooth and there are no lumps
  3. Let rest for ten minutes, then slap and fold
  4. Repeat step three five-six times or until the dough is as smooth as a baby's butt
  5. Cover in plastic wrapping and let rest in a slightly below room temperature place for 12 hours
  6. Cut the dough into six pieces, approx 280 grams each. Fold into balls
  7. Proof separately for another 12 hours
  8. Stretch with your hands. Watch this tutorial
