Moules Frites

Description

Ingredients

  • Mussles - 1 kg
  • White wine
  • Shallotts
  • Thyme
  • Cream
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Fry shallots in butter
  2. Pour wine over shallots
  3. Add mussels (you have of course already washed them)
  4. Make french fries (get a deep fryer and figure it out, it's really not that complicated even though every guy and his mother has an opinion on how it's properly made).
  5. Check out the recipe for aioli and enjoy.
